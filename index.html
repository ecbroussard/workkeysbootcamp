  About the WorkKeys Boot Camp  

When your school or district purchases a WorkKeys Boot Camp, one of our certified instuctors will travel to your school to teach this one-day event to your students.

    • Pacing and time management
    • Test-taking strategies
    • Tips for the most frequently tested question types
    • Applied Mathematics content strategies
    • Reading for Information content strategies
    • Locating Information content strategies
  • The only one-day workshop designed to help students earn their National Career Readiness Certificate™
  • Expert instructors
  • Easy to schedule: during the school day or on the weekend
  • Effective prep in the "final hours" before the test
  • Get more Silver and Gold NCRC® certificates in your school in one day
